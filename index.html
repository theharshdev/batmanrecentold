Beneath the stoic exterior lies a complex and brooding persona, driven by the desire to avenge his parents' death and prevent others from suffering a similar fate. With an unwavering moral code, Batman serves as a symbol of justice, inspiring hope in the hearts of Gotham City's citizens.
Haunted by the loss of his parents to a brutal crime, he adopts the persona of a bat to strike fear into the hearts of criminals.
